The Compound - PEEL Paragraph

In “The Compound” by S.A. Bodeen, I believe that the theme is learning to survive. Throughout the book, you see numerous times Eli talking about running low on certain foods. This shows that at the beginning of the book and toward the end, Eli and his family have to survive on limited types of food. This results in Eli and his family needing to learn to survive with food supplies diminishing. Another piece of evidence is when Eli’s father becomes sick. Eli and his Mom have to learn and use many ways to keep him alive. This included lots of medicine and certain procedures to keep him alive. This was another learning experience with survival. Lastly, is when Eli tries to escape. Eli has to fight off his Dad and use his mind to solve a puzzle of finding out the rest of the code. This is another skill Eli must learn to survive. All three of these pieces of evidence support that the theme in “The Compound” by S.A. Bodeen is learning to survive.
